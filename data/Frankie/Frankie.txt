--------------------- Example Usage -------------------

You can use textures and .blend files (or models, rigs, etc) 
from the data collection, also in commercial projects.
 Make sure we get our credit as mentioned above.

You can use the characters and models to create, distribute
 or sell new games. The originals of the assets you've used
 have to be copied to  your game distribution as well,
including this license.
 You also have to add the attribution in a fair way
(equal to credits for similar contributions to the project).

Contact email for further information: foundation (at) blender.org
